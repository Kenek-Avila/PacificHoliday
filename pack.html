  • Before anything else you should have your Passport* and any other documentation such as a visa, birth certificate, etc.

  • This is a week long trip, be sure to have enough changes of clothes for 7 days plus 2 extra sets in case of accidents. Always better to be safe than sorry!

  • Meals are provided for the morning and evening, but we do suggest to bring snacks and a water bottle.

  • Please bring either cash or card and we can convert up to 300 dollars to the local currency. If you want to bring more, we suggest you to use your local banks services. It is recommended to order the foreign currency a week prior to the trip so that it arrives on time. This money will only be needed for snacks or souvenirs for all commendations will be provided!

  • If you have allergies or require medication, we are not able to provide those resources. Make certain that you have your prescriptions filled prior to the trip to avoid those complications. First Aid will be provided as a necessity in our policy. Once again, better safe than sorry.
